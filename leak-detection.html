FAQ

FAQ: If I want a quote on a project, can I request a no-service-fee visit for an estimate?

Yes! If you decide you want to engage in a project, and need a plumbing service, we can have a plumber tech come out and discuss the details with you, to prepare a formal estimate, at no cost to you!

FAQ: How soon can I get a plumber technician out?

As soon as you call us, we immediately add you to the schedule based on priority listing. If it's an emergency, we will put you at the top of the list, safety first! Our techs work very hard and will most commonly be there within 24 hours at the latest! We take pride in being on-time!

FAQ: Is King Plumber a full service plumbing company?

Great question! Yes, we are full service. We offer inspections, slab leak repairs, line replacements, rough ins, water heater repair, water heater installation, and much more! Whatever you need, we can provide you with a great solution.

FAQ: What areas does King Plumber service?

King plumber services all of the Tulsa Metro, this include Jenks, Bartlesville, Owasso, Tulsa, Broken Arrow, Sapulpa, Bixby, and Sand Springs. If you're just outside these cities, get in touch with us and we will do our best to get a tech out to you as soon as possible.
